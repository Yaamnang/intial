Why is it named LamLha

LamLha or the Chammo Lam is the Tibetan Goddess of Travel and Worldy Protectors. She is said to have rode on the back of a golden bee. As similar to that, our business also helps people in their travels to the country and is also the reason for our mascot to be a bee.
